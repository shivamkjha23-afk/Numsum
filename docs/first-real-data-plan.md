# First Real Data Plan

NumSum Labs starter content should be created manually by the founder from the admin UI. Do not seed fake users, fake MSMEs, fake success stories, fake testimonials, fake impact numbers, or fake metrics.

## Recommended creation order

1. Default MSME onboarding questionnaire.
2. Governance / constitution document.
3. First annual or quarterly objective target.
4. Contribution score rules clearly marked non-equity.
5. One real knowledge asset.
6. One real SOP.
7. One real research item / technology watch item.
8. One draft competition, only if needed.
9. One real MSME challenge, only if available.
10. Pilot or success story only after real validation.

## Content rules

- No fake MSME success stories.
- No fake metrics.
- No fake testimonials.
- No fake company names.
- No fake private customer data.
- Draft content must stay private/admin-only.
- Public content must be intentionally published.
- Public pages may show founding-stage empty states until approved real content exists.
