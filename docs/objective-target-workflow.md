# Objective Target Workflow

Objective targets let NumSum Labs manage annual, quarterly, monthly and sprint objectives as institution-level operating plans.

## Structure

Each target stores a period label, date range, objective summary, strategic theme, progress percent, owners and review notes.

### Workstreams

Workstreams describe execution areas such as MSME Field Intelligence, Problem Statement Lab, SOP & Knowledge Base, Research & Technology Watch, Case Study & Success Story, Solution & Pilot Team and Governance & Documentation.

### KPIs

KPIs track measurable progress with metric type, target value, current value, unit, frequency, owners, evidence requirement and status.

### KRAs

KRAs describe responsibility areas with responsible role, owners, expected outcomes, evidence requirements and status.

## Lifecycle

Targets begin in `draft`, can be approved into `active`, updated with KPI/progress values, completed, paused or archived. In Prompt 6A, access is admin-only and future public/member viewing is intentionally disabled.
