# Public Site Positioning

## Positioning
NumSum Labs is an MSME-first industrial innovation and upgradation ecosystem. It is problem-first, not technology-first: MSME challenges are converted into structured problem statements, knowledge assets, SOPs, research insights, innovation challenges, pilots, and measurable impact.

NumSum Labs should not be described primarily as a social network or generic software platform. It is being built as a long-term industrial innovation institution.

## Homepage Structure
1. Hero
2. What NumSum Labs Does
3. MSME Problem-to-Impact Workflow
4. Key Focus Areas
5. Platform Modules
6. Public Metrics
7. Highlighted Success Stories / Impact
8. Knowledge & Research Highlights
9. Competitions / Innovation Challenges
10. Who Can Participate
11. Call to Action
12. Footer

## Public CTAs
Primary CTA: Submit MSME Challenge. Secondary CTAs: Explore MSME Challenges, Explore Knowledge & Research, View Competitions, Impact / Pilots, and Create Profile / Join as Member.

## Public-safe metric strategy
Homepage metrics use counts from public-safe queries only: public problem statements, approved public knowledge assets, approved/published research items, approved public SOPs, public competitions, and public pilots. If counts are blank or low, the UI uses founding-stage language instead of inflated demo numbers.

## Success story display rules
Success and impact content must be public-safe. Public homepage impact cards currently use public pilot records returned by public pilot queries. Success stories and testimonials may be displayed only when visibility is public and status is published/approved. Raw public comments must not appear without approval.

## Hidden from public navigation
Community, Organizations, Team Directory, Notifications, admin dashboards, internal review, governance, execution, objective targets, and contribution operations are intentionally hidden from the public navigation and footer.

## Final soft-launch homepage standard

The homepage hero should lead with “Turning MSME Challenges into Practical Industrial Solutions,” the MSME-first subheadline, the primary **Submit MSME Challenge** CTA, and secondary exploration CTAs. The network visual should appear below the copy, not side-by-side, to preserve readability and avoid overlap on desktop or mobile.

Public pages should continue using founding-stage language unless real approved public records exist. Do not imply large existing impact, testimonials, or success metrics without verified public-safe data.
