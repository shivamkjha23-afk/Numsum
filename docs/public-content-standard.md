# Public Content Standard

Public NumSum Labs content must be useful, accurate, and safe for MSMEs. Publish only information that is approved for public viewing.

## Public challenge may include

- Industry segment.
- Problem title.
- Public problem summary.
- Process area.
- Challenge category.
- Desired outcome.
- Safe technical context.

## Public challenge must not include

- Private MSME identity unless approved.
- Phone, email, or contact details.
- Internal admin notes.
- Financial details unless approved.
- Confidential process details.
- Unverified claims.

## Public knowledge, research, and SOP content may include

- Practical explanation.
- Public sources.
- Generic industry relevance.
- Safe process improvement guidance.

## Public success story requirements

A success story may be published only after:

- A real pilot happened.
- The result is verified.
- The MSME or partner approved publication.
- Private details are removed.

If approval or verification is unclear, keep the record private.
